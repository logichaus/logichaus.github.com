Some of Our Clients

Emerson Electronics

Logichaus has teamed up with Emerson Electronics, a $24 billion company, to create both JVM-based and iOS applications. Emerson is leading the industry in data center management software and equipment.

 

NASA

We provide training services to NASA at Marshall Space Flight Center in Huntsville, AL. NASA is committed to using and promoting open source software in it's development of new technologies, and our training helps to promote that mission.

 

National Park Service

Logichaus is developing a iOS application that provides enhanced audio and visual tours for NPS vistors. This application supports audio assistance for the visual impaired and visual assistance for the hearing impaired.

Some of Our Products

Off List

Off List lets you read your favorite email lists without filling up your inbox. This application allows you to pull messages from GoogleGroups, YahooGroups, Usenet, and many other sources instead of having them pushed to you by email. You can follow conversations, but remain Off List.

Whiskey Log

Whiskey Log is a journal application that makes it easy to record every scotch, bourborn and rye you've tasted. Search for your whiskey from over 1,000 varities in our database. It includes pictures and regional information on whiskies from Scotland, Ireland, Japan and the USA.
