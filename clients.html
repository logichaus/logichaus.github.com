Some of Our Clients

Emerson Electronics

Logichaus has teamed up with Emerson Electronics, a $24 billion company, to create both JVM-based and iOS applications. Emerson is leading the industry in data center management software and equipment, and Logichaus is helping them get there.

 

NASA

We provide training services to NASA at Marshall Space Flight Center in Huntsville, AL. NASA is committed to using and promoting open source software in it's development of new technologies, and our training helps to promote that mission.
