Joe Kutner

Joe is the founder of Logichaus, and has been working in the software industry as a programmer, architect and consultant for over 10 years. His expertise ranges from Ruby to Java to iOS. Joe has worked for companies of all sizes, and understands the needs the specific needs of each organization. He has worked for IBM with 300,000 employees and Arcturo with 4 employees.

One of Joe's talents is education. He teaches most of our training courses, and has a background in academics. Joe completed his Masters degree in Computer Science at the University of Southern Mississippi, and is working on his CS doctorate at the University of Alabama in Huntsville. He has taught classes for novices and experts in many different subjects.

JRuby is important at Logichaus, and that's in no small part due to Joe. He literally wrote the book on deploying with JRuby. Joe is an expert at porting existing Ruby application to the JVM, and deploying them to less complicated infrastructures.
